About Us

Colette is where European charm meets culinary innovation. Nestled in the heart of Clifton, next door to Xander's, it boasts both indoor and outdoor seating, with a delightful ambiance illuminated by warm lights. Inspired by the flavors of Europe, we continuously craft new and exciting dishes each month, ensuring a fresh experience with every visit. Whether you're seeking a cozy spot indoors or wish to bask in the sunshine on our charming outdoor patio, our cafe promises a memorable culinary journey that celebrates the essence of hospitality and cuisine
